FAQ

  1. Q: What if I want to nominate myself or another amazing woman to be Woman of the Week?

    A: Contact us here!

  2. Q: Where do I start?

    A: If you want to learn more about the gender gap or find ways to empower yourself by learning all about STEM, check out our resources page.

  3. Q: Who can qualify as a woman of the week?

    A: Our mission is to show the world that women have been, are, and will forever be capable of amazing things. We aim to empower everyone who identifies as female or non-binary.

  4. Q: Why are you called She Did That?

    A: “She did that” is a popular slang term used to describe a woman who accomplished something amazing. It’s pretty much the perfect name to describe the women we feature on this site.
